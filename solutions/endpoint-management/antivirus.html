What is Antivirus?

An antivirus is a type of software designed to protect computers and other electronic devices from malicious software which is also known as malware.

Why is it Necessary?

Antivirus software is necessary for businesses to protect their computer systems from cyber attacks and data breaches. With the increasing frequency and sophistication of cyber threats, businesses face a constant risk of malware infections that can compromise sensitive information, disrupt business operations, and cause financial losses. Antivirus software provides a critical layer of defense against these threats, scanning files, emails, and web pages for malware and other malicious code. This is particularly important for businesses that deal with sensitive information, such as financial institutions, healthcare providers, and government agencies. By preventing malware infections and data breaches, antivirus software helps businesses maintain the trust of their customers and avoid reputational damage. Additionally, antivirus software often includes features like real-time scanning, firewall protection, and automatic updates to protect against new and emerging threats.
