Mobile Device Security

January 1st, 2023

by: Colin Yakely

Research Team

What is Mobile Device security?

Mobile devices are any type of device that is, well, mobile. This includes phones, tablets, smart watches, e-readers and much more. These devices have become essential for daily work tasks, as well as for a simple way to carry information from one place to another. The security aspect of mobile devices would be protecting the data on these devices, as well as knowing common tips and tricks to help keep your data secure.

What to Know

With the rise in mobile devices, security for them has become quite important as attackers have Focused their sights on how to exploit them. Some believe that because it is a mobile device, it is secure. While this can be the case, there are still various ways that a mobile device could be breached, or lead to a bigger breach within a company. For example, Apple is a company that makes secure products, but that doesn't mean that these devices can succumb to a breach. This also goes for other companies' products such as Amazon and Google. Some tips below will help you stay safe from a mobile device breach.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Only download reliable apps
  • Do not “jailbreak” your devices
  • Secure Passwords
  • Location settings saved, like “Find my iPhone”
  • Only go to known and secure sites
  • Don't click unsafe links
